System Requirements

Minimum Requirements

  • iOS Version: iOS 15.0 or later

  • iOS Version: iOS 16.0 or later
  • Audio: Headphones or speakers for voice guidance

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start my first release session?

A: Open the “Release” tab, enter your goal, and tap “Start Release”. The app will guide you step by step.

Q: Can I change the app language?

A: Yes. Go to Profile and choose your preferred language in language settings.

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: By default, release data is stored locally on your device. Premium users can optionally enable iCloud sync. If cloud voice guidance is enabled, required text and voice parameters are sent to cloud voice services for TTS generation.

Q: What should I do if the app crashes or freezes?

A: Force close and reopen the app first. If issues continue, restart your device and update to the latest app version.

Troubleshooting

App cannot start or crashes on launch

  1. Force close the app and reopen
  2. Restart your iPhone/iPad
  3. Update app to latest version from App Store
  4. Check available storage space
  5. Reinstall only if needed (local data may be removed)

Cloud voice cannot play

  1. Check network availability
  2. Confirm cloud voice is enabled in voice settings
  3. Retry after switching between Wi-Fi and cellular
  4. Switch to system voice as fallback if needed

Privacy & Data

  • All release records are local by default
  • iCloud sync is optional and user-controlled
  • When cloud sync or cloud voice is enabled, required data is transmitted to corresponding services (such as Apple iCloud, Alibaba Cloud Bailian/DashScope, and Alibaba Cloud OSS)
  • We follow minimum-necessary processing and protect cloud voice file access with access controls and short-lived signed URLs
  • Support requests may include technical diagnostics data, but not your private release content
