Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start my first release session?
A: Tap the "Release" tab at the bottom, enter what you want to release in the text field, then tap "Start Release". The app will guide you through each step of the process.
Q: Can I change the app language?
A: Yes! Go to the "Profile" tab and look for language settings. You can switch between English and Simplified Chinese. The app will automatically detect your system language on first launch.
Q: Where is my release history stored?
A: All your release sessions are stored locally on your device for privacy. You can view your history in the "Profile" tab. Your data is never sent to external servers.
Q: What are the achievement levels and how do I progress?
A: The app includes 15 achievement levels from "Newcomer" to "Liberated". You progress by completing release sessions consistently. Each level requires more releases and represents deeper mastery of the Sedona Method.
Q: Can I edit or delete past release sessions?
A: Yes, you can edit release goals and delete individual sessions from your history. Go to "Profile" â†’ "Release History" and tap on any session to see editing options.
Q: What should I do if the app crashes or freezes?
A: First, try force-closing and reopening the app. If problems persist, restart your device. Your data is automatically saved, so you won't lose your progress.

Troubleshooting Common Issues

App Won't Open or Crashes on Launch

  1. Force close the app by double-tapping the home button and swiping up on SedonaMethodAssistant
  2. Restart your device
  3. Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  4. Check available storage space (need at least 100MB free)
  5. If issues persist, try deleting and reinstalling the app
Note: Reinstalling the app will delete all your local data. Consider this only as a last resort.

Release Sessions Not Saving

  1. Ensure you complete the entire release process
  2. Check that you have sufficient storage space
  3. Restart the app and try again
  4. Verify iOS version compatibility

Language Not Changing

  1. Go to Profile â†’ Language Settings
  2. Select your preferred language
  3. Force close and reopen the app
  4. If still not working, check your device's system language settings
