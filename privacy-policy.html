Privacy Policy

SedonaMethodAssistant

Your Privacy Matters: SedonaMethodAssistant is designed with privacy at its core. All your personal release sessions, emotions, and progress data are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to external servers.

Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

SedonaMethodAssistant stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Release Session Data: Your release goals, identified emotions, desire categories, and session completion records
  • Progress Statistics: Release frequency, achievement levels, and personal milestones
  • App Preferences: Language settings, audio preferences, and interface customizations
  • Historical Records: Past release sessions for tracking your emotional wellness journey

Data We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Any data that could identify you personally

How We Use Your Information

All data collected by SedonaMethodAssistant is used exclusively for:

  • Personalized Experience: Providing customized release sessions based on your preferences
  • Progress Tracking: Helping you monitor your emotional wellness journey and achievements
  • App Functionality: Ensuring the app works properly and remembers your settings
  • Historical Reference: Allowing you to review past sessions and track patterns

Data Security

Your data security is our priority:

  • Local Storage: All data remains on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to cloud services or external servers
  • Device Protection: Data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • No Third-Party Access: No external services have access to your personal release data

Data Sharing

We Do Not Share Your Data: SedonaMethodAssistant does not share, sell, rent, or transfer your personal data to any third parties. Your release sessions and emotional data remain completely private to you.

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Data Access: All your data is accessible within the app's interface
  • Data Deletion: You can delete individual release sessions or clear all data through the app
  • Export Limitation: Currently, data export is not available, but all data remains viewable in-app
  • App Removal: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data

Children's Privacy

SedonaMethodAssistant is designed for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used our app, please contact us immediately.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We will notify users through the app or app store updates
  • The updated policy will be available within the app
  • Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@sedonamethodassistant.com

Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • Apple App Store Privacy Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) principles
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) requirements
  • International privacy best practices
