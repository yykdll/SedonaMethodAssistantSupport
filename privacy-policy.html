Privacy Policy

释放法练习 (Release Practice)

Privacy-first by default: your release records are stored locally on your device. Premium users may optionally enable iCloud sync. If you enable cloud voice guidance, required text and voice parameters are sent to cloud voice services to generate audio.

1. Information We Process

1.1 Local data (default)

  • Release session content (goal, emotion, desire type, answers)
  • History, progress statistics, and in-app preferences
  • Local cache data required for app performance

1.2 iCloud sync data (optional)

  • When enabled by premium users, app data can sync across Apple devices through iCloud
  • This may include release records, settings, and other sync-enabled data
  • iCloud sync is controlled by you and can be disabled anytime

1.3 Cloud voice guidance data (optional)

  • Step text and custom text required for Text-to-Speech generation
  • Voice parameters such as language, voice style, and related model settings
  • Generated audio may be delivered via short-lived signed URLs

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide core release practice features and history review
  • Provide optional iCloud multi-device synchronization
  • Provide optional cloud voice guidance generation and playback
  • Maintain app security, reliability, and troubleshooting support

3. Storage and Security

  • Local storage: data is stored on your device by default using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • iCloud sync: handled by Apple iCloud when enabled, subject to Apple terms and privacy policy
  • Cloud voice: only used when enabled, with encrypted network transport (HTTPS) and controlled file access
  • We apply minimum-necessary processing for optional cloud features

4. Payments and Subscriptions

  • Subscriptions and payments are handled through Apple App Store / StoreKit
  • We do not process your bank card data directly
  • We only receive subscription state required to unlock premium features

5. Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud (optional sync)
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (subscription and billing)
  • Alibaba Cloud Bailian (DashScope) for optional cloud TTS
  • Alibaba Cloud OSS for optional cloud audio storage and delivery

6. Your Controls

  • You can disable iCloud sync in app settings
  • You can disable cloud voice guidance and use system voice instead
  • You can delete local data by uninstalling the app
  • You can manage iCloud data from iOS iCloud settings

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal information, please contact us for removal support.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy when features or legal requirements change. Material updates will be reflected in app updates and/or this page.
